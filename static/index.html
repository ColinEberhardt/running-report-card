An AI-generated report, powered by Strava and GPT

Connect to Strava

The Running Report Card uses your Strava run data to create a personalised report of your running habits. Why not take a look at an example report?. Concept by Colin Eberhardt, and code is all open source on GitHub.

Privacy - The running report card obtains data, with your permission, from the Strava API. This data is not shared with any third parties. Your report, and the raw data returned by the API, are stored in an S3 bucket (a cloud data store). For the purposes of sharing your report, this S3 bucket is public, however the application itself does not share reports - what you do with your report, and who you share it with, is up to you! if you do wish to have any of your data removed, please contact Colin Eberhardt (colin.eberhardt@gmail.com).
